Our Story

Tasty Pizzais a pizza restaurant which started by three Afgan brothers
named Amir Ahemed and Nissam. They started this restaurant
in the memories of there elder sister Asna. She was an
excellent pizza maker and she taught her brothers how to make pizza.
Her dream was to start a pizza restaurant with her brothers. But
she can't fullfill her dream. She died in a car accident. Her brothers
fullfilled her dream by starting this restaurant.

We are offering you a wide variety of pizzas and its toppings.
Our restaurant is one of the main pizza restaurant in Toronto.
We are providing home delivery to the customers with low
delivery charges as soon as possible when the order placed. The most interesting thing is
that we give free delivery to the customers who ordering pizza that cost more than$20.
